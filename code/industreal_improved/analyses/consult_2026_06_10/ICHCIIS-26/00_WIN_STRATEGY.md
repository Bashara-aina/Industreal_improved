## How to Win at ICHCIIS-26

### Your 3 Key Advantages

1. **Technical depth**: Your paper has real engineering (ConvNeXt-Tiny, 5-task architecture, x402 blockchain). Most ICHCIIS papers are surveys or UX studies.

2. **Ethics framework**: The IEEE 7005-2021 ethical analysis is a complete, standards-grounded contribution that HCI conferences value highly.

3. **Real deployment**: This is not a simulation — you built and tested on actual hardware (RTX 3060, Solana devnet). That is rare at this venue.

### The 3 Things to Emphasize

1. **The human problem first**: Open with the fair compensation problem, not the architecture. HCI reviewers care about PEOPLE, not models.
2. **The ethical framework as contribution**: Frame the IEEE 7005-2021 analysis as the primary contribution, the system as evidence.
3. **The cost democratization**: "$299 vs $10,000+" is a narrative anyone can understand.

### The 3 Things to Downplay

1. **Detection mAP**: Don't lead with it. Put it in a results section with honest framing (fine-grained state discrimination).
2. **Blockchain technical details**: Keep x402/Solana to 1 paragraph unless the reviewer is interested.
3. **Training details**: Don't describe staged training in depth. 1 sentence.
